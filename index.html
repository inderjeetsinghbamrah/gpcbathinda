Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda

Government Polytechnic College Bathinda was established in the year 1985 to impart quality education in the field of Engineering and Technology,with an aim to cater to the students of the southern Punjab. This College is situated on Bibiwala road approx 3kms from Bathinda bus stand and approx 4.2kms from Bathinda railway station junction. It has been established on a lush green campus of 22 acres.

Scholarships Offered
GPC Bathinda offers a vide variety of scholarship schemes to its students.
In order to uplift and encourage the students with good academic record belonging to General Category, Govt. of Punjab Launched CM Scholarship Scheme in the year 2017 in Government Colleges Offering Admissions to First Year and LEET Entry to Diploma Courses in various streams including Technical and Pharmacy Courses. (Notification). Several Students till date have been benefited from this scheme.
Read More....

The Objective of this scheme re-launched by Government of Punjab is to appreciably increase the Gross Enrollment Ratio of SC Students in higher education with a focus on those from the poorest households, by providing financial assistance to SC Students at post-matriculation or post-secondary stage who re domiciled in Punjab and have done matriculation from State of Punjab including Chandigarh to enable them to complete their education.
Read More....
Several Scholarship schemes have been launched by the Central Government during recent years. The objective of these schemes is to provide financial assistance to the students who are not able to secure their education in the conventional manner. The eligibility conditions and other norms aligned with the State/ UT of which the student is a domicile are to be followed.
Read More....
Punjab State Board of Technical Education (PSBTE) Chandigarh has launched several scholarship schemes for students to provide financial assistance in achieving their education goals in technical education.
Read More....
The objective of the scheme is to motivate the students belonging to OBC category and to provide financial assistance to these students coming from background with low income and belonging to non-creamy layer. Students belonging to OBC can apply for scholarship on Dr. Ambedkar Scholarship Portal at https://scholarships.punjab.gov.in
Read More....
All India Institute of Technical Education also offers few scholarships for different category students through National Scholarship Portal portal.
