Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda

Government Polytechnic College Bathinda was established in the year 1985 to impart quality education in the field of Engineering and Technology,with an aim to cater to the students of the southern Punjab. This College is situated on Bibiwala road approx 3kms from Bathinda bus stand and approx 4.2kms from Bathinda railway station junction. It has been established on a lush green campus of 22 acres

Principal's Message
Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda was established in the year 1985 to impart practical and quality education along with sufficient job opportunities in the field of Engineering and Technology. The Institute has been awarded with the Outstanding Institution Award (2013-14) and the Best Polytechnic Award (2008-09) by the Government of Punjab.
