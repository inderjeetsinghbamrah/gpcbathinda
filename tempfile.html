Various scholarship Schemes

GPC Bathinda offers a vide variety of scholarship schems to its students.

CMS Scholarship

There are so many students in the state who are good in academic, but they don't have the good sources to complete their studies. Now any student in Punjab, who wants to complete technical or medical education with the financial assistance of Government can easily complete their studies without facing any hassle. Fee Waive off Ratio in Scholarship Scheme- Students who have scored 60% to 70% in class 10 will get the 70% of Tution fee waive off. Students who have scored 70% to 80% in class 10 will get the 80% of Tution fee waive off Students who have scored 80% to 90% in class 10 will get the 80% of Tution fee waive off Students who may have scored 90% above will get the 100% of Tution fee waive off.

SC/ST Scholarship Scheme

The students of SC Category of Punjab shall pay only Rs. 1133/- at the time of admission, subject to condition that annual income of his family is less than Rs. 2,50,000/-

Fee Waiver Scheme

Full tution fee waiver is given to 5% students in each branch, subjected to condition that annual income of his family is less than Rs. 8,00,000/-
