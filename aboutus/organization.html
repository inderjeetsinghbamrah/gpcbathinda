About Organization

Government Polytechnic College, Bathinda was established in 1985 to impart quality education in the field of Engineering and Technology, with the aim of catering to the students of southern Punjab. The college is located on Bibiwala Road, approximately 3 km from the Bathinda bus stand and about 4.2 km from the Bathinda railway station junction. It is spread across a lush green campus of 22 acres, featuring meticulously planned buildings and well-landscaped grounds.
In addition to imparting technical education in various trades, the college focuses on the following key objectives:-

To provide skilled technical manpower to meet industry requirements.
To train students for self-employment and entrepreneurship.
To place special emphasis on the overall personality development of students by encouraging participation in extracurricular activities and competitions.

The institute boasts experienced and well-qualified faculty members across all departments, who are dedicated to enhancing students' skills through both theoretical and practical knowledge. Special attention is given to academically weaker students, with optimal solutions provided to improve their learning outcomes.

Students can avail themselves of various scholarships offered by the institute. Regular extracurricular and sports activities are organized to ensure holistic development. The institute also hosts over eight clubs under the Student Recreational Committee, aimed at helping students enhance their personal and professional skills.

The college has a well-established Training and Placement Cell that provides comprehensive support in securing employment opportunities for eligible and deserving students. Almost every year, all eligible students are successfully placed in reputed organizations and industries. Students from this institute have brought laurels in various fields.
