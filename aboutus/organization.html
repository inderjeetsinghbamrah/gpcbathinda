About Organization

Government Polytechnic College Bathinda was established in the year 1985 to impart quality education in the field of Engineering and Technology, with an aim to cater to the students of the southern Punjab. This College is situated on Bibiwala road approx 3kms from Bathinda bus stand and approx 4.2kms from Bathinda railway station junction. It has been established on a lush green campus of 22 acres. It has meticulous planned buildings and well landscaped grounds.
The focus of the college in addition to imparting technical education to students in their various trade is the following:-

To make available technical man power to the industry as per their requirements.
To train students for self employment & entrepreneurship.
To lay special emphasis on overall personality development of the students by encouraging them to participate in extra curricular activities & other competitions.

Institute has experienced and well qualified faculty in all the departments who always focus on improving the skills of students through theoretical as well as practical knowledge. The special attention is always given to the weak students and optimal solutions are always provided to improve their learning abilities.

Students can avail huge benefits through different scholarships available. Extra Curricular Activities and Sports Activities are conducted on regular basis to develop the overall personality of the students. Institute through its more than 8 clubs under Student Recreational Committee, always strive to help students to enhance their skills.

Institute also has well established Training & Placement Cell to provide every possible assistance to provide employment opportunities to all the eligible and deserving candidates. Almost every year all the eligible students are placed in renowned organizations/ industries. Students from this institute has brought laurel in many fields.
