Anti Ragging Committee

Anti Ragging

Ragging is any act resulting in Mental/ Physical/ Sexual Abuse, Verbal Abuse, Indecent Behavior, Criminal Intimidation/ Wrongful Restraint, Undermining Human Dignity, Financial Exploitation/ Extortion and Use of Force on new entrants or junior students by the senior students.
Ragging is totally banned in the College premises and anyone found guilty of ragging and/or abetting ragging is liable to be punished as it is a criminal offense. The Institute follows a zero tolerance policy in case of any ragging activity is reported. In case of emergency the students / parents in distress owing to ragging may contact the following:
