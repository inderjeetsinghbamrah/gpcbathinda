SRC Clubs

SRC Clubs

In order to promote the overall growth of the students and to bring out the best of the students, Institute has started initially with Six Club Literary Club, Art & Hobby Club, Health Club, Cultural Club, Eco Club, E-Club. Later Four More Clubs which include Personality Development Club, Science Club, Logical Reasoning Club and Research and Innovation Club were added to hone the skills of students in diverse areas.



Literary Club

The aim of this club is to encourage the students to become orators and display their intellectual, independent thinking skills and imbibe a sense of confidence. The other aim of this club is to induce reading habits in students and enhance their literary skills and to provide a platform for debating events.

Art & Hobby Club

The aim of this club is to participate in developing the college students in forming their personality with balance and integrity. The primary objective of this club is to inspire students to pursue a hobby and explore their potential areas like Painting, Rangoli, Collage Making, Slogan Writing, Designing etc.

The Art & Hobby Club includes various activities like Painting, Sketching, Collage Making, Calligraphy, Best out of Waste, Sculpture Making, Poster Making, Stone and Face Painting, Rangoli Design and Slogan Writing.

Cultural Club

Cultural club is one among the SRC clubs. This club is formed to discover the inner talent of students, moreover it plays an important role in the enhancement of individuals emotional, cognitive and social development. The club activities include singing, dancing, poetry and speech competitions etc. Such events help to bring out the interests, abilities, opportunities and strengthen their personality traits.

E-Club

The aim of E-Club is to conduct activities for students using electronic media , as a fun way of assessing knowledge and improving their skills like audio-visual quizzes, finding movie messages etc. Primary aim of this club is to conduct digital extra curricular activities as the way of promoting e-learning and to help students develop creative thinking, memory retention and listening skills.

Personality Development Club

Personality development helps you gain recognition and acceptance from the society as well as people around. Personality development plays an essential role not only in an individual's professional but also personal lives. It makes an individual disciplined, punctual and an asset for his/her organization. The aim of Personality Development is to hone the inherent skills of the students and make them able to communicate effectively.

Science Club

Science club is a better forum for the students to realise their quest in pursuing scientific activities for which they do not get opportunities in their regular classroom teaching. Science club aims:

1. To promote general interest in Science among students
2. To inculcate scientific attitude and scientific temper.
3. To encourage individual and group activities.
4. To keep the students in touch with the recent advances in science.
5. To provide challenging opportunities to the gifted students.

Research & Innovation Club

Research and innovation (R&I) plays an essential role in triggering smart and sustainable growth and job creation. By producing new knowledge, research is central to developing new and innovative products, processes and services, which enable higher productivity, industrial competitiveness, and ultimately prosperity. The aim of this club is to provide a platform to inculcate basic research practices, tools helpful in designing and innovating in students at early stages.
